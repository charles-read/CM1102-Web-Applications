Enter Year to find the Easter Sunday Date


Easter is the Christian celebration of the anniversary of the Death and Resurrection of Jesus Christ. This happened on a
date representing the first Full Moon of Spring on the Jewish Calendar. Because of that, Easter Day is always the first
Sunday after the first calculated Full Moon on or after March 21st.

Format Presentation
Numerically dd/mm/year (e.g. 20/4/2003).
Verbosely Day/of/named_month/year (e.g. 20th of April 2003).
Both Both of these formats combined.
