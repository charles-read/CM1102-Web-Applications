Andreessen's Impact

The impact Andreessen that has made in previous years are still relevant today. The software, ideas and technology he has developed in the last few decades have molded
how we use the internet as well as how we access our resources. Without Netscape, the browser market would have been delayed or been more Microsoft focused which may have
resulted very differently. For example, without Netscape, many internet browsers would probably still have a big price tag on them. Also, graphical
internet browsers allowed the casual home owner to browse the internet, until that point, the world wide web was mainly used in military, education and business.

As well as internet browsers, other software packages like Opsware shaped the way things would be in the future. Although Opsware was for businesses,
it was the first high performance cloud based service which is similar to the cloud based technology we have today, but
of course, it has developed a lot over the last few decades. Overall, the developments and ideas
Andreessen has developed and encouraged has impacted the way technology businesses function today.
